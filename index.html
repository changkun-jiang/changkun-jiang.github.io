Changkun Jiang (江常坤)

  Changkun Jiang

Networked Systems; Game Theory and Mechanism Design; Optimization and Learning.

Email: jiangchangkun@gmail.com


Changkun Jiang received his Ph.D. in Information Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in June 2017, and worked as a postdoc at CUHK until moving to Shenzhen in June 2018. His research interests primarily revolve around designing intelligent mechanisms for resource sharing and allocation in networked systems, by using analytical tools from network games, mechanism design, optimization, and learning. More recently, he is also interested in artificial intelligence and its applications in interdisciplinary areas such as bioinformatics.
