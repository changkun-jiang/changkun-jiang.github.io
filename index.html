Changkun Jiang (江常坤)

  Changkun Jiang

Networked Systems; Game Theory and Mechanism Design; Optimization and Learning.

Email: jiangchangkun@gmail.com


Changkun Jiang (S'13-M'18, IEEE) received Ph.D. in Information Engineering (Advisor: Prof. Jianwei Huang) from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in June 2017, and worked as a postdoc at CUHK until moving to Shenzhen in June 2018. His research interests are primarily in the area of designing intelligent resource sharing and allocation mechanisms for networked systems, by using analytical tools from network games, mechanism design, optimization, and learning. Currently, he is also interested in artificial intelligence and its applications in the interdisciplinary areas such as computational biology and bioinformatics.
