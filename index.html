Email: jiangchangkun@gmail.com;  ckjiang@szu.edu.cn


Hello! I am an Associate Professor of Computer Science and Software Engineering at Shenzhen University, China. Previously, I received my Ph.D. in Information Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK).
My research interests lie broadly in networked systems and computing, with a particular focus on cloud-edge-client collaborative network resource management using analytical tools such as network games, mechanism design, optimization, and learning. More recently, I have broadened my interests to explore artificial intelligence and its applications across various interdisciplinary domains.
